About Us

Hingeder is a dating service that works to match you with your perfect life partner! Our state of the art matching algorithm is guaranteed to help you find someone that Here at Hingeder, we pride ourselves on being a business founded by a few friends who wanted to make it easier to meet new people. We are a small team of developers that met in college and decided to start a business together. What started as a class project has turned into a full fledged business that we are passionate about continuing to build and improve. We are constantly working to improve our product and welcome any feedback. We want to make your experience using our service the best possible, and we can only do that if we listen to our users. We hope you enjoy using Hingeder as much as we enjoyed building it!


Our Mission

When we first came up with the idea of creating a dating consultant business, we thought what will distinguish our business from the others? We decided that the best way for us to stand out was to create a service that felt genuine and authentic. When you decide to use our service, you will be matched with a real person who will help you find the best match for you. We want it to feel like you are interacting with a friend that wants to see you find the perfect person for you. We took aspects from our own experiences talking to our friends about dating and put them into our service. We hope that you will feel a sense of comfort and trust when you use our service like you do with a close friend.
